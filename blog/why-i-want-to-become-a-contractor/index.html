Why I want to be a contractor

Hello everyone,

This is my first English blog post. In this post, I will try to explain why I want to be a contractor and the way how I will become a contractor.

There is one thing in my life which is the key thing makes me ever happy is continuous learning and what keeps me motivated is challenging. I was a child, I wanted to be a scientist. Which is not a good career path in my country. I chose software development. Somehow I found them similar.

I learnt programming before college and I even made money from it. It was a freelancing job and I had a good salary for a teenage boy. Then I co-founded a business with two of my best friends and it was even greater than freelancing. Even it was not a very successful business, we learnt a lot from it made lots of fun. During my career, by luck or my personal curiosity, I had to choose a different technology for each project I worked on and I had to learn each programming language or framework from scratch. Perhaps, It was the only way to take the control over my hunger for learning, trying and discovering new technologies.

During college and after college, I kept trying different technologies again and again. When I discovered Ruby and Ruby on Rails, I fell in love with them. The clean syntax and mature ecosystem helped to adapt very quickly. I built projects and had so much fun with RoR.

I tried various design patterns with various programming languages. I tried both object oriented programming and functional programming approaches. I developed desktop applications. I worked as both frontend and backend developer roles. I am not specialized on any specific technology. Luckily I have a taste of many software development technologies and that helped to become a very adaptive developer.

My dear friends, it has been more than 10 years since I started my first company. And I also worked as a regular employee in a couple of companies. Finally, I realized that there is no perfect world that I can find a company to work at that I can be fully qualified and become useful in a logical sense with my polyglot experiences(Of course I mean in my city.). That’s why I have decided to become a contractor. I want to share my skills and experiences with a company which really demands them from me.

Fortunately, I found TopTal as a chance to give a try to achieve my goal. To sustain my learning hunger, to help companies to digitize their ideas and to work at whereever I want. I want to become a contractor. Not just an ordinary contractor, also be one of the top Toptal Ruby on Rails Developers.

Thank you all for reading.

Turhan Coskun
